Accidential Compensation Corporation

Time scale: June 2016–Jan 2017

PwC digital

ACC began as a limited compensation scheme in the 1900. Its purpose was to help workers who suffer injuries where there is no directly responsible party. In 1967 the New Zealand government extended the act to include all citizens injuries on a no-fault basis. ACC are the sole provider of accident insurance coverage in NZ.

My role

In 2016, PwC digital were commissioned to transform ACC which included all digital channels including its website.

I was part of PwC digital in 2016. My responsibilities were in interaction and experience design. I worked alongside other designers of mixed skillsets. Each designer at PwC digital was required to understand the fundimentals of human-centred design and be capable of utilizing a methods toolkit of activities to help our clients achieve both there business and user outcomes.
