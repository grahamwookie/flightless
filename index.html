Usability testing – to lab or not?

Since adding User Testing to my oeuvre of skills, I have often wondered about laboratory user testing and how at times can feel very contrived. Could this mean test findings aren’t as close to the truth as we’d ideally like.

In laboratory user testing, you essentially have a room where users are observed interacting with a prototype which is being evaluated for its usefulness.
